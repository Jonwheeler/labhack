LabHack Travel

Here you will find directions, a map, a list of local restaurants and hotels for those who do not wish to hack through the night or take advantage of complimentary meals. For those wishing to stay at one of the local hotels listed here, please mention to the hotel staff that you will be participating/observing the AFRL Labhack event. You will be given a military rate regardless if you are active duty military, a student, or a company representative.
