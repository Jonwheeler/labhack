The Air Force Hackathon

and Mini-Conference

Join us for LabHack, the first Air Force hackathon. This is a chance to show off your programming, design and data science skills by building solutions to real-world challenges that AFRL researchers encounter every day. We provide the food, location, big network pipelines and challenges. There's cash prizes, workshops, talks, swag and fun events along the way. This event is open to the public.
  • What: A 26-hour hackathon around sensor data
  • When: Saturday, 25 October 2014 - Sunday, 26 October 2014
  • Where: WBI Tec^Edge Innovation and Collaboration Center, Dayton, OH
  • Why: To learn something new, build something awesome and have fun!
  • Who: This event is open to the public. Anyone, military or civilian, may participate.
For more questions and answers, see our FAQ.

Hackathon Registration Day Pass Available!

LabHack sold out of hackathon tickets 59 days in advance, but you still have a chance to attend. By popular demand, we're offering a Day Pass which provides access to:

  • All talks and workshops
  • Lunch
  • Dinner
  • Snacks
  • Swag and goodies

You'll also be able to meet some of the hackathon participants and experience the excitement of LabHack and the wonderful people and organizations involved.

Doors open at noon for Day Pass holders on Saturday, 25 October 2014. We ask that Day Pass holders do not arrive prior to noon in order to give hackathon participants time to brainstorm, pitch, form teams and start hacking.
