Join us for LabHack, the first Air Force hackathon. This is a chance to show off your programming, design and data science skills by building solutions to real-world challenges that AFRL researchers encounter every day. We provide the food, location, big network pipelines and challenges. There's cash prizes, workshops, talks, swag and fun events along the way. Best of all, this event is 100% free and open to the public.
  • What: A 26-hour hackathon around sensor data
  • When: Saturday, 25 October 2014 - Sunday, 26 October 2014
  • Where: WBI Tec^Edge Innovation and Collaboration Center, Dayton, OH
  • Why: To learn something new, build something awesome and have fun!
  • Who: This event is open to the public. Anyone, military or civilian, may participate.
